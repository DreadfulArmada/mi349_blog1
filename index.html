Is The Sopranos better than Breaking Bad?

Is The Sopranos better than Breaking Bad? I think there is really an argument to be made here. Tony Soprano is one of the best characters in all of television. Tony is a complex character that we really only come to understand towards the end of the show. The Sopranos set the stage so that Breaking Bad would be able to be so good. Without The Sopranos, Breaking Bad would not even be a good show. In fact, when watching Breaking Bad they give nods to The Sopranos in a couple of scenes.

The Sopranos really set the stage for TV dramas. It was one of the first and best types of TV dramas to come out. IMBD sets The Sopranos as the tenth best show of all time. I think that theres something to be said for it being the tenth best show. All of the characters are very well written and fleshed out. Chris and Adriana are one of the most iconic couples of all time, and I don't care if you disagree. You can't argue with the facts

Either way, bost shows are very good in their own right. Both are blockbuster hits and should be regarded as so. Tony and Chris are just as equal as Walt and Jessie. Both duos are very compelling characters to watch.
